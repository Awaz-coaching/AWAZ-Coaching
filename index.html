About Me

Hi, I’m Ahmad Walid. My fitness journey started in the most unexpected way – a simple shopping trip for a cool T-shirt. But when I tried it on, I realized that my body didn’t match the look I wanted. That moment sparked a determination in me to change. I started with simple ab workouts in my room, and within two weeks, I began to see results. This motivated me to join the gym, and from there, I never looked back.

As I continued to train, bodybuilding became a major part of my life. It brought discipline, focus, and a sense of achievement. My dedication led me to compete in bodybuilding competitions, and in October 2022, I won the IFBB Cup of France, qualifying for international competitions. Although I narrowly missed my Pro Card at the IFBB Diamond Cup in December 2022, coming in second, the experience fueled my passion even more.

My desire to share my knowledge and experience with others grew stronger over time. In 2023, I began the journey to become a certified personal trainer. After a year of intense study and preparation, I obtained my BPJEPS diploma in July 2024, officially allowing me to coach clients. Now, I am dedicated to helping others achieve their fitness goals with the same passion and commitment that has driven me.

As a coach, your goals are my goals. I create personalized training programs and nutrition plans tailored to your unique needs and objectives. Together, we will work to achieve the results you desire, whether it's building muscle, losing weight, or improving overall fitness.
